Tour du Mont Blanc

France, Switzerland, and Italy

Ranking as a top travel experience by both my wife and me, this hike circles the Mont Blanc massif while crossing through the alps of three different countries. We stayed in mountain huts and feasted on alpine comfort food to replace the thousands of calories we burned each day. The 105 mile trek has an elevation gain and loss of almost 33,000 feet!
